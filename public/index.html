About

Before discovering the awesomeness of programming, I was an educator at the high school level. I taught Art I, so I dealt with students of all levels of interest and skill in the subject. I enjoyed teaching, getting to know them, and seeing the 'light bulb' when someone 'got it.'
In programming, I seek the challenge of solving a puzzle. The how of solving it is fun, and refactoring even more so. I revel in learning something new each day, and meeting that next task head on!
I enjoy a good hike, traveling to new places, reading books with my cat, Daniel, and making things of all kinds. I frequent craft fairs, maker spaces, and meetups. In the arts, I specialize in watercolor with ink.
